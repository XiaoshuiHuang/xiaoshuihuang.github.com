About

I'm a PhD student at Oxford University, broadly interested in machine learning and computer vision. I am lucky to be advised by Professor Andrea Vedaldi in the VGG Group. My research focuses on semi-supervised and multi-modal learning.

I've also recently started an undergraduate machine learning research group for undergrads getting started in the field. If that sounds interesting to you, please visit our website and join our Slack.

I'm always interested in meeting new people and hearing about potential collaborations. If you'd like to work together or get in contact with me, please email lukemk-at-robots-dot-ox-dot-ac-dot-uk.
